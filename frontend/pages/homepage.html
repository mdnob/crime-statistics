L.A. Crime Data Visualizer

Created by: Matthew Nobleza, Jack Goldstein, and Neil Shah

This website is designed to visualize and analyze crime data in Los Angeles. Explore different queries and gain insights into crime patterns and trends. The available trend queries include:

  1. Crime Normal Curve: Visualizes the distribution of crime victim ages over time, showing a normal curve.
  2. Crime vs Unemployment: Examines the correlation between crime rates and unemployment rates in the region.
  3. Crime vs Inflation: Analyzes the relationship between crime instances and inflation levels.
  4. Per-100k Crime Rates: Displays crime rates normalized per 100,000 population, providing a standardized comparison.
  5. Crime Rates on Popular Holidays: Explores top 10 crime hotspot areas in L.A. during popular holidays.
